Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a plain spaghetti cooked in combination of minced meat, tomatoes and spices, prepared in a short time and enjoyable.

view Reviews

Ingredients

  • 6 big Plum Tomatoes
  • 500g Beef Mince
  • 1 big Onion
  • 1 cooking spoon Vegetable Oil
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 leaves Bay leaf
  • 1 tbsp dried Thyme
  • 1 tbsp all-purpose Seasoning or mixed Spice
  • A small finger/chunk Ginger
  • Handful Scent leaves/Basil/Effirin
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Water for cooking

Directions

  1. Put tomatoes, half of the onion, ginger, and scent leaves into a blender and puree untill smooth.
  2. Place a frying pan on fire and add the oil. Once it is heated, add the garlic and the remaining onions (chopped), then saute till translucent.
  3. Add the minced meat and stir to combine and cook till the brrf browns all over.
  4. Pour the blended tomatoes into the beef and add the remaining ingredients. Pour in a cup of water and cover to cook for at least 25-30 minutes. Add more water if needed.
  5. Once cooked, you will get a thick looking tasty sauce. Check for taste and bring down.
  6. Meanwhile, boil water and cook your spaghetti with a little salt till it is done, then drain.
  7. To mix your bolognese sauce with the spaghetti, scoop some of the sauce into a pot of spaghetti and set on medium heat. Combine well, simmer for a few minutes and bring down.
